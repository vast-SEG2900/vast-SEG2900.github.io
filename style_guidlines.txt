Style Guideline
---------------

Theme of webpage:
---------------
Our website will have a futuristic theme, incorporating dark colours and glow across different pages. This theme will help emphasize our product and company goals.

Color Theme:
------------
The colour scheme of our website will include:
-Black and dark shade for the background.
-Glowing shades blue and cyan. #53eafd, #000000, #4dd0e1
-White for words
-Hints of purple for different icons or aspects of every webpage.  

   
Typography:
-----------
-Neutral font 
-Varying sizes for titles, descriptions, and sections.

Buttons
--------
Purchasing Buttons will have rounded edges and have a glowing design using the chosen colors outlined above.
